# 🏠 Visualizing Housing Market Trends

### An Analysis of Sale Prices and Features Using Tableau

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project, developed for **ABC Company**, focuses on uncovering trends and insights from housing market data to understand what drives **house prices and sales performance**.

By analyzing features such as **renovation age, house characteristics, and overall quality**, the project supports **strategic pricing decisions and market competitiveness**.

Interactive visualizations created in **Tableau** allow stakeholders—including **real estate analysts, marketing teams, and executives**—to explore and interpret complex housing data effectively.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze how **renovations impact house sale prices**
* Examine **house age distributions** based on bathrooms, bedrooms, and floors
* Provide a **data-driven view of the housing market** for better decision-making
* Visualize housing trends in an **interactive and accessible format**

---

## 🔍 Key Scenarios & Visualizations

### ✅ Scenario 1: Overall Data Overview

* Displays the **count of transformed housing data records**
* Shows **average sale price** and **total basement area (sq. ft.)**
* Provides a quick understanding of **dataset scale and key metrics**

---

### 🛠 Scenario 2: Total Sales by Years Since Renovation

* Histogram of **total sales grouped by years since renovation**
* Reveals how **recent renovations influence house sale prices**

---

### 🏘 Scenario 3: Distribution of House Age by Renovation Status

* Pie chart showing **house age distribution based on renovation status**
* Helps assess the **age of housing inventory and renovation prevalence**

---

### 🛁 Scenario 4: House Age by Bathrooms, Bedrooms, and Floors

* Grouped **bar chart categorizing house age by key features**
* Identifies patterns in **buyer preferences and housing characteristics**

## 🧹 Data Preprocessing Steps

* Removed **duplicate records**
* Handled **missing renovation year values**
* Converted **categorical values into dimensions**
* Created calculated measures:

  * **Years Since Renovation**
  * **House Age**
* Aggregated **sale prices for grouped comparisons**
* Connected the transformed dataset to **Tableau Desktop for visualization**
